About Serenity Suite

Serenity Suites is the most preferred hotel in West Africa, and it is all about the right mix! Located in the heart of Victoria Island, we offer our clients a perfect blend of business & leisure amenities with dining and recreational options delicately infused in one amazing space. We crown all these with services that meet the highest international standards.

Water Scenery

Experience the tranquility of our hotel's exquisite water features, where nature and luxury blend seamlessly to create a serene haven. The gentle sound of cascading waterfalls fills the air, while our pristine infinity pool appears to merge with the endless horizon of the ocean beyond. Whether you’re relaxing by the poolside with a refreshing drink, strolling along the waterfront pathways, or watching the vibrant colors of the sunset dance across the water, every moment by the water promises to rejuvenate your senses. For those seeking adventure, enjoy kayaking or boat rides along the shimmering river.

Our stunning water scenery offers not just a view but an immersive experience of peace and relaxation—perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration or simply embracing a moment of calm reflection.
